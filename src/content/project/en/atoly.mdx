Atoly is the main SaaS product I have been shaping through Basaltbytes.

The product is designed for independents and small teams who need bookings,
payments, and partner flows in one platform. The technical work centered on
tenant isolation, OAuth-based sign-in, and a backend that stays simple to
operate while supporting product iteration.
