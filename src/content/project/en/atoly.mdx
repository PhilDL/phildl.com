Atoly is a booking and scheduling platform I built for creative studios and
small service businesses — pottery workshops, art classes, cooking lessons, and
similar activity-based businesses. Owners get an online booking page, a
back-office to manage schedules and customers, and integrated payments, all
without any technical setup on their side.

Under the hood, the platform runs as a Turborepo monorepo with a Hono backend,
React 19 front-ends, and isolated Turso databases per tenant. Real-time
availability is computed from weekly schedules, booking rules, and connected
Google/Outlook calendars. Payments flow through Stripe Connect, and Inngest
handles asynchronous workflows like email/SMS reminders and calendar sync.
A companion mobile app is built with Expo.

I own every layer of the product: ideation, positioning, marketing, customer
support, architecture, and day-to-day engineering.
