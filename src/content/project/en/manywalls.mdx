Manywalls is a digital exhibition platform created for artists who work with
physical pieces — paintings, sculptures, drawings. Instead of grid portfolios,
artists compose full-screen, linear exhibitions wall by wall, each wall
presenting a single artwork at proper scale on a textured, colored background.

The mobile app handles exhibition creation: artists photograph their work, and
an OpenCV pipeline detects the painting boundaries, corrects perspective, and
cleans up the background. They then sequence walls — artwork, text, or
intentional silence — with gesture-driven reordering and real-time preview. The
web experience delivers exhibitions as immersive, scroll-snap pages stripped of
all UI chrome, designed for juries, galleries, and grant committees.

The whole system ships from a Turborepo monorepo: two Expo apps (Manywalls and
PaintingLens), a TanStack Start marketing site, a public exhibition viewer, an
admin dashboard, and a Convex real-time backend — deployed across App Store,
Play Store, and Cloudflare Workers.
